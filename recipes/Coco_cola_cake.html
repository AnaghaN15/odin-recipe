Coco Cola Cake

Description

Chocolate coca cola cake with a rich pecan frosting.

Ingredient

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ teaspoon bakingsoda
  • ½ teaspoon ground vanilla essence
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup cola-flavored carbonated beverage
  • 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cups miniature marshmallows

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  2. Make cola cake: Combine flour and sugar in a mixing bowl. Place butter, cola soft drink, and cocoa in a saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil. Pour over flour mixture; mix well.
  3. Stir in marshmallows, buttermilk, eggs, baking soda, and vanilla until well combined. Pour batter into the prepared dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean, about 40 minutes. Spread cola frosting over hot cake.
  5. Make cola frosting: Combine butter, cola soft drink, and cocoa in a saucepan; bring to a boil. Pour over confectioners' sugar in a large bowl; mix well. Stir in chopped nuts.
